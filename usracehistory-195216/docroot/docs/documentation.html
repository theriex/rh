The goal of the U.S. Race History Project is to provide timelines that promote understanding and respect for the context of people whose histories are frequently ignored. To maximize impact, points are presented in a compelling format with minimum required time commitment.

This site was motivated by a perceived need for historical context, coupled with the difficulty of knowing where to start.

All timelines are incomplete. You are encouraged to check all points for yourself. Knowledge evolves over time and history has multiple perspectives.

Points and Timelines

Points for Timelines are contributed by member organizations. A subset of all points are chosen for general quick reference. All points are public and can be used by anyone to create a timeline. All timelines are public and can be read by anyone.
